Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Elias Crew Co. ("we," "us," or "our") provides home-improvement and remodeling services in the McKinney and Dallas–Fort Worth area. This policy explains what information we collect when you contact us — including through the chat assistant on our website and through Facebook Messenger — and how we use and protect it.

Information We Collect

When you reach out to us through our website chat or our Facebook Page on Messenger, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number and/or email address
  • Your city or service area
  • Details you share about your project (for example, the type of work, size, or timeline)
  • The content of the messages you send us

We only collect information that you choose to provide in the course of asking about or requesting our services. We do not ask for sensitive personal information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your questions and messages
  • Contact you to discuss your project and schedule a free estimate or consultation
  • Follow up about services you have asked about
  • Improve how we communicate with and serve our customers

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers that help us operate, and only as needed to do so:

  • An artificial-intelligence provider that helps generate the chat assistant's responses to your messages.
  • An email and notification provider that delivers your inquiry to our team so we can follow up.
  • Meta Platforms, Inc., when you contact us through Facebook Messenger, as part of providing that messaging service.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Facebook Messenger

If you message our Facebook Page, your messages and the information you provide are handled according to this policy. Your use of Messenger itself is also governed by Meta's own terms and privacy policy.

Data Retention

We keep the information you provide only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, provide our services, and maintain ordinary business records, after which we delete or anonymize it.

Your Choices and Data Deletion

You may request that we provide, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time. To do so, contact us using the details below and we will respond promptly. You can also stop messaging us at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our services and messaging are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
